About the Game

Price of Glory: Tactics puts players in command of forces where planning outweighs aggression.

You've got to read the field like a chess master - figure out what your opponent's planning before they do it. Charging forward without preparation can quickly lead to losses, while a patient strategy can turn even a disadvantageous position into a win.

Campaign scenarios introduce new tactical challenges, forcing players to adapt rather than rely on a single approach.

Gameplay

01
Turn-Based Command
Players alternate actions with opponents, giving you time to think things through before you make your move. Movement, positioning, and timing all influence the outcome of engagements.
02
Terrain Advantage
The terrain totally changes how you play. Elevated positions, narrow routes, and defensive structures can completely change the game - smart positioning beats just throwing units at the enemy.
03
Unit Coordination
Don't put all your eggs in one basket - even your strongest unit needs backup.
